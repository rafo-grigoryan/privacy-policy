📄 Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile application ("App") handles user data.

1. Information Collection

We do not personally collect or store personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise locations. The App does not require account creation and can be used without registration.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

The App uses third-party services, specifically the Google AdMob advertising system. AdMob may automatically collect non-personal and device-related data, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifiers (Advertising ID)

This data is used to display advertisements, limit ad frequency, and select relevant ads in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

3. In-App Purchases and Payments

The App offers in-app purchases (donations and a one-time payment to unlock features/remove advertisement requirements). All financial transactions are processed securely through the Google Play Billing system. We do not collect or store your financial or credit card information. Google Play provides us only with purchase confirmation and purchase history to enable the premium features within the App.

4. User Choice and Control

Users can restrict advertising tracking through their device settings using the iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT) system or the Android "Limit Ad Tracking" option. Users can also completely bypass the need to watch rewarded advertisements by making a one-time in-app purchase.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data ourselves, it is not stored on our servers. Data processed by third-party services (Google Play and Google AdMob) is protected according to their respective security policies. All data transmitted through these services is encrypted in transit.

6. Children's Privacy and Families Policy Compliance

We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. Since the App is accessible to all age groups, including children, we strictly comply with the Google Play Families Policy. We configure our advertising SDKs (Google AdMob) to serve non-personalized and family-safe ads to child users, limiting data collection as required by applicable laws (such as COPPA).

7. Use of Advertisements

Advertisements displayed in the App are strictly optional (rewarded ads) and do not interrupt the core gameplay against the user's will. Users have the choice whether to watch an advertisement to receive specific in-game rewards or continue the game normally.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes become effective from the moment they are published within the App or on our official store listing page.
