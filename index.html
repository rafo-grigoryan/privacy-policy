📄 Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile application ("App") handles user data.

1. Information Collection

We do not personally collect or store personal data such as name, email address, phone number, or location. The App does not require account creation and can be used without registration.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

The App uses third-party services, specifically the Google AdMob advertising system. AdMob may automatically collect non-personal and device-related data, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifiers (Advertising ID)

This data is used to display advertisements, limit ad frequency, and select relevant ads in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

3. User Choice and Control

Users can restrict advertising tracking through their device settings using the iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT) system or the "Limit Ad Tracking" option.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data ourselves, it is not stored on our servers. Data processed by third-party services is protected according to their respective security policies.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to intentionally collect personal data from children. If such a case occurs through third-party services, it is governed by the policies of the respective service.

6. Use of Advertisements

Advertisements displayed in the App are optional (e.g., rewarded ads) and do not affect the core functionality of the game. Users can continue the game without being required to watch advertisements.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes become effective from the moment they are published within the App.
